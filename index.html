Biology Game

Points:

Oil:
This is the amount of oil you have drilled. After a couple seconds, it will be burned and covert to the next stage.

Carbon in Atmosphere:
This is the amount of oil/carbon that has been burned, and is now in the atmosphere. After a few seconds, a tree will grow, using the carbon in the air. This requires at least 2.

Plants:
Through the process of photosynthesis, carbon enters the plant. The carbon is fixed into carbohydrates, such as glucose. After a while, an animal will eat the plant, ingesting the carbon.

Animals:
After eating the plant, the animal will ingest the carbon. After a while, the animal will return that carbon to the ground, creating oil, starting the cycle over again.
